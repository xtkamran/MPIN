    About MPIN Security

    An MPIN (Mobile Personal Identification Number) is used to authenticate mobile banking transactions. A strong MPIN should:

    • Not be a commonly used sequence (e.g., 1234, 1111)
    • Not be based on personal information like birth dates
    • Not be repeated digits or simple patterns
    • Be memorized but not easily guessed by others
